About Janice Blake, M.A.

Janice Blake, M.A., is a licensed psychologist with a Master of Arts in Clinical Psychology from Marshall University Graduate College. She has dedicated her career to serving individuals and families in central West Virginia, with a specialization in diagnosing and treating chronic mood and psychotic disorders.

Credentials

Professional Experience

Janice Blake has worked extensively in individual, family, and couples therapy. She has provided psychological evaluations for courts and agencies, supervised psychologists, and held leadership roles in private practice.

Services Offered

  • Individual Therapy for Adolescents & Adults
  • Family & Couples Therapy
  • Psychological Evaluations
  • Forensic & Court-Ordered Assessments
  • Supervision for Psychologists
