In Case of Emergency

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out to one of the following resources:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness: 1-801-323-9900 or 1-877-230-6264
  • Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration: 1-877-SAMHSA-7 (1-877-726-4727)
